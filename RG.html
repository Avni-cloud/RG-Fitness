Who's RG ?

Rohit Ghaghat(RG) is Pune's most famous bodybuilder,having begun in the year 2012. The Pune-based bodybuilder has won numerous titles which include Puneshree and Kothrudshree (85Kg category) at the 2012 Interstate Bodybuilding Championship in Pune, Maharashtra.

All the success came with hard work at the gym. The bodybuilder trains others to get in shape and states that there is no age limit when it comes to bodybuilding. RG’s dedication to bodybuilding and fitness, as well as helping others with their fitness has made him very successful and one of Pune’s most famous bodybuilders.
